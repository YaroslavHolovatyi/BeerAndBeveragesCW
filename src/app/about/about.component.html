About This Tavern-Finder App

Hear ye, adventurer!

Every great quest begins in a tavern.

And so we forged this app — a magical tome created to guide heroes, wanderers, and thirsty souls to the finest pubs across the realm.

Whether thou art seeking a quiet corner for storytelling, a rowdy hall of ale, or a legendary tavern that only appears to worthy adventurers…
— this app is your map, your companion, and your quest log.

Pages of the App

List of Pubs

A grand codex of all taverns known to the realm.

Browse through every bar, study its specialties, and choose your next destination. For every pub is a chapter, every visit a tale.

Random Pub

Can't choose your next tavern?

Invoke the Spell of Random Selection, and the app shall send you to a pub chosen by fate itself. The Fates consider:

  • your race,
  • your tastes,
  • your past quests,
  • your earned achievements.

A perfect choice for indecisive parties and chaotic adventurers.

Check Time — Split the Bill

In every tavern, the final battle is the Bill of Many Numbers.

This page lets you record every drink and feast ordered by your party:

  • flagons of ale,
  • enchanted cocktails,
  • roasted boar,
  • mystical snacks…

Assign each item to each adventurer and let the app calculate who owes what. No need for dice rolls or disputes — the spell handles it all.

Achievements

As in any good adventure, your deeds shall be rewarded. Achievements grant you:

  • renown,
  • unique badges of honor,
  • better deals in certain taverns,
  • and race-exclusive perks.

Some achievements are open to all heroes of the realm.

Others are sacred and granted only to specific races — for example:

  • Dwarves earn titles for ale mastery.
  • Elves unlock honors by tasting fine wines.
  • Orcs gain glory through mighty feasts.
  • Halflings excel at visiting cozy taverns and sampling every dessert.

Collect achievements to rise in status and unlock secret taverns and offers.

Races & Your Drinking Destiny

Upon entering the realm, each adventurer chooses their Race.

This affects:

  • preferred taverns,
  • favorite potions (drinks),
  • recommended routes,
  • unique achievements,
  • bonuses for exploring the world.

Your race shapes your drinking destiny.

Elves savor elegant wines, Dwarves seek deep cellars of ale, Humans try everything, and Tieflings… well, they drink things not meant for mortals.

As you journey, the app remembers your tastes, choices, and victories — crafting a personalized map of taverns suited exactly for your character.
